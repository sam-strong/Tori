Chef::Formatters::Minimal

Formatters::Minimal

Shows the progress of the chef run by printing single characters, and displays a summary of updates at the conclusion of the run. For events that dont have meaningful status information (loading a file, syncing a cookbook) a dot is printed. For resources, a dot, S or U is printed if the resource is up to date, skipped by not_if/only_if, or updated, respectively.

Attributes

updated_resources[R]
updates_by_resource[R]

  else
    puts "\n"
    puts "resources updated this run:"
    updated_resources.each do |resource|
      puts "* #{resource.to_s}"
      updates_by_resource[resource.name].flatten.each do |update|
        puts "  - #{update}"
      end
      puts "\n"
    end
  end
end
